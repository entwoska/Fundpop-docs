Method 1: Installing from the Onboarding Flow

  1. After installing the app, you'll see a button to open the Shopify theme editor

Click on the Open customization page button to open your theme editor

  1. Click the button to open the theme editor in a new window - the Fundpop widget will automatically be added to your default product template

Make sure the Default product template is selected and position Fundpop to your desired location

  1. In the editor, drag and drop the Fundpop block to your preferred position on the product page
  • Recommended placement: underneath product title and price
  1. Click save before closing the window
  2. Return to the onboarding screen
  3. Click the Continue button when you see the installation confirmation to begin setting up your first campaign

Method 2: Installing Directly from Theme Editor

If you can't access the onboarding flow or prefer manual installation:

  1. Go to your Shopify Admin â†’ Online Store section

Open the theme editor for the desired theme

  1. Select your theme and click Customize
  2. Select the Default product template at the top of the theme editor

Make sure the Default product template is selected

  1. In the left sidebar, locate where you want to place the widget:
  • Hover over the desired location
  • Click the + icon between blocks
  • Search for "Fundpop widget" in the apps section

Search for "Fundpop" and add the Fundpop Widget from the Apps section

  1. Click save to complete the installation
